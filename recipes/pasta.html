Classic Pasta Recipe

Description

This classic pasta dish is a simple and flavorful meal featuring al dente pasta tossed in a rich tomato-based sauce with garlic, herbs, and a hint of spice. Itâ€™s perfect for any occasion and easily customizable with your favorite add-ins.

With minimal ingredients and ready in just 25 minutes, this pasta is ideal for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal. Serve it with grated Parmesan and fresh basil for an extra touch of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil and Parmesan cheese for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese. Serve hot and enjoy!
