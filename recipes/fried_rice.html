Classic Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This classic fried rice is a quick and flavorful meal, combining rice with vegetables, eggs, and savory seasonings. It’s a fantastic way to use up leftover rice, transforming it into a satisfying dish that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side.

Ready in under 20 minutes, this recipe is endlessly customizable with your choice of protein and veggies, making it perfect for a busy weeknight meal or a last-minute gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup peas and carrots (frozen or fresh)
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat half the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove from pan and set aside.
  2. Add remaining oil to the pan and sauté onions until translucent.
  3. Add peas and carrots, cooking until tender. Stir in the rice and cook, stirring, until it begins to crisp slightly.
  4. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and add soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Toss everything together until well combined.
  5. Garnish with green onions and serve hot. Enjoy!
