noodles

Description

This noodle dish features tender noodles and crisp vegetables tossed in a savory, garlic-infused sauce with hints of soy and sesame. Quick and satisfying, it’s perfect for weeknight dinners.

Easily customizable, you can add your favorite protein or extra veggies to make it your own. Ready in just 30 minutes, it’s a flavorful, versatile meal that’s sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (rice noodles, egg noodles, or your choice)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and onions, sautéing until fragrant.
  4. Add bell pepper, broccoli, and carrots, cooking until tender-crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and sugar. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir well.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet, tossing to coat with the sauce and vegetables. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until noodles are heated through and fully coated.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with green onions or sesame seeds if desired. Enjoy!
